    Access All Areas Energy

    There are 1.1 billion people worldwide living without electricity and many more with limited or heavily disrupted supply. This means closing your shop at sunset, sitting indoors with dangerous kerosene lamps, and not being able to do your homework.

    Flexible, Enabling Technology

    Solar is a clean and efficient way of providing electricity to those who live on and off-grid. But buying solar panels outright is far too expensive for many people and a simply out of reach for those living without power.

    Expanding Energy Horizons

    Cloud Solar’s smart monitoring system and innovative hardware technology enables energy providers to offer flexible technologal and payment solutions – including pay-as-you-go. Cloud Solar reduces financial and connectivity barriers to energy access, whilst increasing security and scope of provision for energy providers.

    The Cloud Solar System

    Cloud Solar allows energy providers to sell energy in a smart, flexible and secure way – whilst increasing the reach of their services. The system has a modular hardware design, meaning lower costs and further possibilities. Data is transferred from the hardware to our cloud server and displayed on our management platform.

    We are happy to create a bespoke system for your needs, whether you work on or off grid, with solar home systems, mini-grids, or anything in between.

    Software

    Our management portal allows users to see their system performance and pay their bills via a web or mobile interface, whilst energy providers can remotely view and operate the systems. The Cloud-Solar portal provides an easy way for customers to communicate with providers.

    Hardware

    We can provide our monitoring hardware for small DC systems up to mini grids. A ‘hub’ and ‘node’ system keeps Cloud Solar online; whether you are connected to WiFi or live in an area with no internet or phone signal. Our modular design means that you can add or remove features, depending on your needs and budget.

    Key Features

    Reduced hardware costs

    We use a ‘hub’ and ‘node’ model to reduce costs to our clients. A ‘hub’ can serve an area of up to 225 km2, communicating with up to 2000 more simple nodes. Data transfers from ‘nodes’ to more complex ‘hubs’ before being sent to the server.

    Unlimited payment options

    Our system allows the project owners to create their own payment plans, whether based on flat rates, pay per unit, time based tariffs, or a combination. The way customers are charged is 100% flexible.

    Use in unconnected areas

    We use a range of advanced communications technology to send data from unconnected regions, into connected areas and to our servers. This means Cloud Solar allows control of solar technology just about anywhere.

    Connect in with your maintenance team

    You can set customised alerts to be sent to your HQ or field agents, by SMS, email or push notifications, to ensure your repair and maintenance operations are responsive and effective.
