    The Cloud Solar product suite allows project owners to offer their services in a smarter way that involves lower overheads, lower investment risks, flexible tariffs and payment plans.

    Co-funded by Innovate UK and CORFO (Chile), Scene (UK) and Ciudad Luz (Chile) have developed Cloud Solar to allow the Energy Services Company (ESCo) model to be offered to users of solar energy. This is applicable to on-grid users, who choose to use solar energy, and also for the rural and urban poor; for whom Cloud Solar lowers barriers to accessing energy services.
    Cloud Solar has a modular hardware design, acknowledging the diversity of needs that exists within the solar energy sector – we are happy to create a bespoke system for your needs.

    Cloud Solar integrates smart monitoring, control and payment technologies.

    Management Portal

    Our system allows users to pay as they go for solar energy, whilst providing technology owners the overview and control that they need to ensure that their operations run smoothly. Our management portal allows users to see their system performance and pay their bills via a web or mobile interface, whilst system owners can remotely view and operate the systems.

    Hardware

    Our hardware has a modular design, meaning that you can add or remove features, depending on your needs and budget.

    Key Features

    Reduced hardware costs

    We use a hub and node model to reduce costs to our clients. Only one hub is required in an area of [X] KM2, with data transfers from simple nodes, to more complex ‘hubs’ before being sent to the server.

    Flexible payment options

    Our system allows the project owners to create their own payment plans, whether based on flat rates, pay per unit, or any other method.

    Use in unconnected areas

    We use advanced communications technology to send data from unconnected regions, into connected areas and to our servers. This means Cloud Solar allows control of solar technology just about anywhere.

    Connect in with your maintenance team

    You can set customised alerts to be sent to your HQ or field agents, by SMS, email or push notifications, to ensure your repair and maintenance operations are responsive and effective.
